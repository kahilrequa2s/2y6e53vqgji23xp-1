<p><ul><li>Helium-flow</li><li>Liquid Helium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum cryostat market can be categorized primarily into two types: helium-flow cryostats and liquid helium cryostats. Helium-flow cryostats utilize a continuous flow of helium gas to maintain low temperatures, offering efficient cooling for various applications, including research and industrial use. In contrast, liquid helium cryostats rely on stored liquid helium to achieve and maintain cryogenic temperatures, providing stable and low temperature environments, ideal for delicate experiments and superconducting materials. Both types play crucial roles in cryogenic applications.</p></p>
