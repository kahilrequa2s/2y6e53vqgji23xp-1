<p><ul><li>Brewed Soy-based Sauce</li><li>Blended Soy-based Sauce</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy-based sauce market can be categorized into two main types: brewed soy-based sauce and blended soy-based sauce. Brewed soy sauce is produced through fermentation, utilizing soybeans and wheat, resulting in a rich umami flavor. It often features complex aromas and is favored in traditional Asian cuisines. In contrast, blended soy sauce combines brewed soy sauce with additional ingredients, such as seasonings or preservatives, to create a more versatile product that caters to diverse culinary applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
