<p><strong>The Casing Elevators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Door</li><li>Counterpart</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Casing elevators are essential tools used in the oil and gas industry for transporting casing pipes during drilling operations. The two primary types are side door and counterpart casing elevators. Side door casing elevators feature a hinged side door, allowing for easy loading and unloading of pipes without needing to remove the entire elevator. In contrast, counterpart casing elevators consist of two matching halves that clamp around the pipe, providing a secure grip. Both types enhance efficiency and safety in well construction tasks.</p></p>
